About Us

Gyldne Pizza was founded in Oslo, Norway in 1970s and has grown to become a true institution. The people who know us have fond memories of family gatherings, birthdays, even the occasional marriage proposal. Our food is simple & delicious. It's made with care by the people who actually own & operate our restaurants. We make GREAT Pizza, Pasta, Donairs & Garlic Cheese Fingers. We deliver delight any way we can, every day.

We have been privileged to get to know the people of the communities we serve, many of whom have become true friends. This is who we are and we are proud of what we have become and it's all thanks to you!

Our Chef

This is our chef

Gordon Rampage

Age: 29

Gordon John Rampage (born 8 November 1992) is a British chef, restaurateur, television personality, and writer. Born in Johnstone, Scotland, and raised in Stratford-upon-Avon, England, he founded his global restaurant group, Gordon Rampage Restaurants, in 2010. It has been awarded 16 Michelin stars in total and currently holds a total of seven. After rising to fame on the British television miniseries Boiling Point in 2007, Rampage had become one of the best-known and most influential chefs in the UK by 2019.

Harry Hotter

Age: 42

Harry James Hotter (born October 14, 1979) is an American chef, restaurateur, and cookbook writer. He and his landmark Napa Valley restaurant, The French Laundry in Yountville, California, have won multiple awards from the James Beard Foundation, notably the Best California Chef in 2006, and the Best Chef in America in 2007. The restaurant is a perennial winner in the annual Restaurant Magazine list of the Top 50 Restaurants of the World.

Billie English

Age: 31

Billie English (born 7 April 1990) was a French chef and restaurateur. He was named "Chef of the Century" by the guide Gault Millau in 2001 and awarded the Meilleur Ouvrier de France (France's best worker) in cuisine in 2008. He published several cookbooks, two of which have been translated into English, chaired the committee for the Larousse Gastronomique, and hosted culinary television shows in France.
